# LANDING_ARCHITECTURE.md — v0.1

**Rigor — Marketing Site Structural Architecture Specification**

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the structural architecture of the Rigor marketing site.

It establishes:

* Narrative order.
* Section hierarchy.
* Functional role of each block.
* Content density constraints.
* Conversion logic consistent with protocol positioning.

The landing must reflect structural discipline.
It must not resemble SaaS marketing.

---

## 2. Architectural Principles

The landing must:

* Communicate inevitability, not persuasion.
* Present structural thesis before features.
* Establish conceptual territory before explanation.
* Avoid feature-driven sequencing.

The page is declarative.

---

## 3. Structural Flow Overview

The landing follows this ordered structure:

1. Hero
2. Structural Acceleration Thesis
3. Structural Entropy Definition
4. Protocol Introduction
5. Architectural Position
6. Core Invariants
7. Differentiation
8. AI Positioning
9. Call to Action

Each section has a specific cognitive role.

---

## 4. Section Specifications

---

### 4.1 Hero Section

**Objective:** Establish category and authority.

Structure:

Primary:

RIGOR
AI Constraint Protocol.

Secondary:

Formal boundaries for AI-generated systems.

Optional tertiary:

Generation requires boundaries.

CTA:

* Read the Manifesto
* Explore the Specification

No feature claims.
No visual noise.

---

### 4.2 Structural Acceleration Section

**Objective:** Establish inevitability.

Content pattern:

* AI increases generation velocity.
* Structural validation does not scale proportionally.
* Unbounded generation produces entropy.

This section introduces the problem without dramatization.

---

### 4.3 Structural Entropy Section

**Objective:** Define the owned concept.

Include:

Formal definition of Structural Entropy.

List of manifestations:

* Implicit transitions
* Unclassified evolution
* Migration ambiguity
* Contract instability

This section legitimizes the category.

---

### 4.4 Protocol Introduction Section

**Objective:** Introduce Rigor as structural response.

Structure:

* Define AI Constraint Protocol.
* State that it operates pre-execution.
* Emphasize boundary function.

No implementation details.

---

### 4.5 Architectural Position Section

**Objective:** Clarify systemic role.

Include diagram:

```
Human Intent
      ↓
RIGOR — Constraint Protocol
      ↓
AI Generation
      ↓
Execution Systems
```

Explicitly state:

Rigor is upstream of orchestration.

---

### 4.6 Core Invariants Section

**Objective:** Establish non-negotiables.

List:

* Explicit transitions
* Deterministic outcomes
* Version-classified evolution
* Validation precedence
* Structural boundedness

Short declarative statements.

No extended explanation.

---

### 4.7 Differentiation Section

**Objective:** Prevent misclassification.

Clarify separation from:

* Temporal
* AWS Step Functions
* HashiCorp Terraform

Language must remain structural.

No competitive tone.

---

### 4.8 AI Positioning Section

**Objective:** Clarify relationship with AI.

Structure:

* Humans define intent.
* AI generates implementation.
* Rigor constrains structural validity.

Avoid claims of enhancement or optimization.

---

### 4.9 Final Call to Action

**Objective:** Direct toward deeper material.

Primary options:

* Read the Manifesto
* Explore the Protocol Specification
* Access Documentation

No sign-up pressure.
No urgency language.

---

## 5. Visual Behavior Guidelines

* Large vertical spacing between sections.
* Strong typographic hierarchy.
* Minimal color usage.
* Diagrams preferred over decorative imagery.

The landing must feel structured, not dynamic.

---

## 6. Content Density Constraints

* Avoid long paragraphs.
* Prefer short declarative blocks.
* Use structural separators.
* Avoid feature grids.

No pricing section.
No testimonials.
No social proof.

Rigor does not rely on validation by popularity.

---

## 7. Conversion Philosophy

The landing does not optimize for conversion rate.

It optimizes for conceptual clarity.

Conversion is intellectual alignment.

Users who resonate will proceed to documentation.

Users seeking tooling convenience will self-select out.

---

## 8. Long-Term Adaptability

The landing may later include:

* Playground demonstration
* Specification examples
* Interactive structural diagrams

However, the structural order must remain:

Problem → Constraint → Boundary → Invariants → Differentiation.

---

## 9. Structural Integrity Clause

The landing must never degrade into:

* Feature marketing
* Startup growth narrative
* Productivity claims
* Emotional storytelling

Any such change violates identity coherence.
